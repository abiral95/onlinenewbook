    "## Conservative and reactive transport systems ## \n",
    "\n",
    "**Conservative transport system** is when there is\n",
    "- no interaction with aquifer solid material,\n",
    "- no interaction with other chemicals,\n",
    "- no interaction with living organisms (e.g., bacteria)\n",
    "\n",
    "```{warning}\n",
    "When either of the above interaction is part of the process, then the system is **reactive**.\n",
    "```"
   ]
